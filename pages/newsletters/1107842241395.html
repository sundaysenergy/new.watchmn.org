WATCH annual Gala  

Wednesday, November 30, 2011


Greetings! ,

 

Planning for our annual gala is underway and we hope you will join with us to sponsor this event.   

 

As a sponsor, we ask you to lend your name to the invitation, invite your friends, colleagues, and family members to attend, and make a sponsor gift. Just fill out the sponsor form and email it to the WATCH office.

 

We're delighted that Tom and Mary Racciatti will host the gala at their spectacular rooftop home in Humboldt Lofts, next to the Mill City Market. Spoonriver restaurant owner and award-winning chef Brenda Langton will provide catering.  

 

Gala is a celebration of all the work we have accomplished this year, and a time to recognize and thank the individuals from the community, like you, who make our work possible. This year, Lieutenant Nancy Dunlap will share examples of WATCH's impact on improving the court's response to sexual assault, and you'll hear from us about our vision for the coming year. 

 

Please respond by Friday, September 30, 2011. We hope you'll join in hosting this year's gala, and we look forward to seeing you at the Racciatti's.

 

Many thanks,

 

Susan Lenfestey                                   Marna Anderson

Founder                                                Executive Director
