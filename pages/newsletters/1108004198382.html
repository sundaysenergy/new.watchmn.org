NACMP Member Releases Report!   

Congratulations to Court Watch Montgomery on the release of their report, Protecting Victims of Domestic Violence in Montgomery County:Challenges and Opportunities with Protective and Peace Orders.

  

Court Watch Montgomery has been a member of the National Association of Court Monitoring Programs since 2010, and received technical assistance from WATCH.  

 

It is a great report that is sure to have a positive impact on the courts in Montgomery County. We are proud of them and of our role in helping them reach this milestone!
