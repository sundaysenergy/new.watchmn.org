ART OF RECOVERY 

Minnesota victims or survivors of crime who have expressed their experience or recovery through an artistic medium are invited to submit their art or literary work for inclusion in the Eighth Annual Art of Recovery exhibition.  

 

Indviduals of all ages who have used the arts to respond or heal from crime are encouraged to enter. You do not need to be an "artist" to apply.  

 

Deadline:  

Entries must be postmarked by Thursday, November 10th or emailed before 4:30 pm on Monday, November 14th.
