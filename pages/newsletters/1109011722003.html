Dear friends,

You count on WATCH to be in court every day evaluating and improving how cases of violence against women and children are handled. By donating to WATCH, you can become part of the solution to making our courts safer and more fair. Your gift will help us:

 

Get guns out of the hands of abusers

Federal law prohibits people convicted of domestic assault from possessing guns, but the law is generally ignored. It shouldn't be. Firearms are the weapon most frequently used in intimate partner homicides, and WATCH is leading the effort to enforce these statutes.

 

Ensure defendants are given appropriate probationary conditions 

WATCH research found that pre-sentence investigations are not completed for many defendants convicted of domestic violence, in violation of Minnesota law. To effectively supervise domestic abuse defendants and keep women safe, WATCH is working to ensure the courts follow the law.

 

Weapons screening in all Hennepin County courthouses

Suburban courts do not have weapons screening, which can put the lives of court staff and the public in danger. WATCH believes that everyone has the right to be safe when entering our courthouses and is working in conjunction with community leaders to make this happen.
