March is Women's History Month!
What will you do to celebrate and honor the progress we have made?
March 8th is International Women's Day.
Thousands of events are held throughout the world on this day to celebrate women's achievements. This year's theme is Connecting Girls, Inspiring Futures.

Free Screening of "Crime After Crime"  This documentary depicts Deborah Peagler's legal battle after being imprisoned for life for connection to the murder of her abuser. Don't miss it on   March 13th.

Occupy Women's Rights!
Join OccupyMN on March 24th to rally and celebrate the many struggles of women for democratic rights. Share ideas about how to protect women's rights now and in the future, and remember victories from the past. Details here.

Ending Violence Against Women Symposium
Hear three speakers, including attorney Nadia Costa who will speak about her effort to free Debra Peagler from life imprisonment for murder of an abusive spouse. This event will be held Tuesday, March 27th.

 Thanks to Your Note
Last month after returning from a shift at Domestic Violence court, WATCH monitor Randy expressed his concern over victim advocates playing with their phones and seemingly "just hanging out" in the courtroom. Because of Randy's feedback, WATCH staff followed up with the advocate supervisor at the Domestic Abuse Project, informing her of the perception of advocates in the courtroom. Thanks to your notes, we can work at keeping all aspects of the justice system professional, effective, and responsive.
