Attention all Seward Co-op Shoppers!

WATCH has applied for the Seward Co-op's SEED donation program and wants your help! Through the SEED donation program, customers can "round-up" the totals of their purchases or add a donation to their grocery amount, and the money will be donated to the organization that is that month's SEED recipient.

Being chosen to participate in this program would not only be an incredible opportunity for WATCH to raise money (most recipients receive between $6,000 and $16,000), but it would also be a new way to inform the community about who WATCH is and why we matter.

So how can you help? If you shop at the Seward Co-op, put in a good word for WATCH next time you're in. Or consider asking a staff member how you can share your input on deserving organizations. Do you know someone who works there? Tell them about WATCH and the work that we do and encourage them to keep us in mind.

The SEED recipients will be decided at the Co-op's July meeting, so if you make it there before then don't forget to mention WATCH. In fact, you can continue to mention us every time you go in!

Never been to the Seward Co-op? It's worth the trip. Learn more about their SEED program here.
