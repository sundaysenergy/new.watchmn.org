 June 29, 2012 
 
Dear Hennepin County Judicial Officers:
 
WATCH is again offering the opportunity for you to receive confidential and individualized feedback based on monitors' observations. In 2009, WATCH initiated a judicial feedback project in which monitors' comments on courtroom decorum were collected and sent to the 34 judges who opted to participate. 

Please email Kate Hovde by Friday, July 27 if you are interested in participating or have any questions or concerns. We will begin collecting notes on August 1 for a period of one year. Feedback will be sent on a quarterly basis in the form of an email attachment.
Sincerely,
Kate Hovde
Court Monitoring Coordinator
612-341-2747, ext. 2
khovde@watchmn.org
  
WATCH is a court monitoring and research organization in Hennepin County. WATCH's mission is to make the justice system more effective and responsive in handling cases of violence, particularly against women and children, and to create a more informed and involved public.
