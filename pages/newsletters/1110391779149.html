20 Reasons to Host a Movie Night
1. You believe in WATCH's work.
2. You love, or even just like, movies.
3. You're a pre-law student, law student, or are just interested in the law . . . and you have friends who are too.
4. Normally you don't feel like you can ask people for money because you don't know anyone with much of it. Now that donations will be doubled, it feels doable.
5. You remember hearing about the hot coffee lawsuit, but aren't really sure what happened.
6. You saw the Seinfeld episode about hot coffee.
7. You're not sure what "tort reform" means.
8. You believe it's important for the courts to be accountable to the public.
9. Any excuse to eat popcorn is a good one.
10. That book club never got off the ground.
11. You want an opportunity to tell your friends and family about WATCH.
12. It could be the new thing you learn that day.
13. It's like getting a free DVD rental.
14. You have friends without air conditioned apartments so they'd be guaranteed to attend.
15. You're the hostess with the most-ess.
16. You want to learn how Americans sometimes give up their constitutional rights without knowing it.
17. You have an espresso machine you've been dying to use and you see a theme party on the horizon.
18. You're a socially-minded individual.
19. The politicization of judicial elections scares you.
20. You want to support WATCH in new (and fun!) ways.
Help WATCH Double Our Money!
You can help WATCH
continue to have a daily presence in the courts by hosting a
WATCH Movie Night.

Our goal is to get 30 WATCH supporters to host a screening of the award-winning documentary Hot Coffee for their own friends and family. This film, which starts by looking at the famous McDonald's coffee case, takes a closer look at tort reform and our civil justice system.

Hosting a WATCH Movie Night is an opportunity to share why you think a public presence in the courts is important, and to encourage people to donate to WATCH.
All donations from new donors will be doubled.

Our deadline is Labor Day. Contact Mandy if you're interested. We'll provide you with a copy of the DVD as well as a movie night care package.
