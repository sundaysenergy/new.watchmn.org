July 23, 2012
 
Dear Hennepin County Judicial Officers:
 
At the end of last month, you received an email inviting you to participate in WATCH's judicial feedback project. Beginning in August, we will collect monitoring notes for individual judges who opt to participate. The notes will be summarized and sent confidentially via email attachment at quarterly intervals for a period of one year. It is a great way to learn more about WATCH and see what our monitors are documenting while in the courtroom.

I'm writing to remind you that the deadline for responding is this Friday, July 27th. Please contact Kate Hovde with any questions or to indicate your interest. We hope to hear from you!

For those who previously responded, please disregard this reminder. I have recorded your interest. Thank you!  
Sincerely,
Kate Hovde
Court Monitoring Coordinator
612-341-2747, ext. 2
khovde@watchmn.org
  
WATCH is a court monitoring and research organization in Hennepin County. WATCH's mission is to make the justice system more effective and responsive in handling cases of violence, particularly against women and children, and to create a more informed and involved public.
