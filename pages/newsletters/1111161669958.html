WATCH
Annual Fall Gala November 29, 2012

Greetings!

Fall is here, which means cool weather, warm sweaters and the annual WATCH Gala!

 

This year's Gala will be held on Thursday, November 29, 5:30 to 7:30 pm, at the home of WATCH founder and board member Susan Lenfestey. We hope you will celebrate with us as we enter our 20th year of monitoring the courts.


WATCH's accomplishments over the years are many, and their impact stretches well beyond the courthouse and the county.

 

In one example of WATCH-at-work, when Hennepin County held its first sex trafficking trial this year, WATCH monitors were in court to document the outcome. WATCH is collecting data to evaluate the impact of new legislation on stemming the tide of prostitution and sex trafficking. The action WATCH takes is local, but the impact has no boundaries.

 

Gala is a celebration of all the work we have accomplished this year, and a time to recognize and thank the individuals from the community, like you, who make our work possible.

 

We hope you will sign on to host this year's Gala, and will encourage others - especially those who may not know of WATCH - to join you on November 29th. WATCH parties are good parties!

 

Please return the RSVP form by October 12, or send your reply to jparrott@watchmn.org, indicating how you would like your name(s) to appear on the invitation.

 

We can't thank you enough for your continued support and we look forward to seeing you on November 29th.

 

With gratitude,

 

                                                           

Susan Lenfestey                        Donna McNamara      

Founder and Board Member          Acting Executive Director
