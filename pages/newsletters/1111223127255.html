 Join the Host Committee for the WATCH Gala
Greetings!

 

WATCH's annual Fall Gala is a celebration of all the work we have accomplished this year, and a time to recognize and thank the individuals from the community, like you, who make our work possible. This year's Gala is also your opportunity to meet Amy Arcand, the new executive director at WATCH.  

 

We hope you will celebrate with us as we enter our 20th year of monitoring the courts. Gala will be held on Thursday, November 29, 5:30 to 7:30 pm, at the home of WATCH founder and board member Susan Lenfestey.   

   

Please return the RSVP form by October 12, or send your reply to jparrott@watchmn.org, indicating how you would like your name(s) to appear on the invitation. We hope you will also encourage others - especially those who may not know of WATCH - to join you on November 29th.   

We can't thank you enough for your continued support and we look forward to seeing you on November 29th.

 

With gratitude,

   

Susan Lenfestey                                 Donna McNamara      

Founder and Board Member                 Acting Executive Director
