Last Call to RSVP for Volunteer Appreciation!

The event will be held at WATCH board member Kate Kelly's home from 5-7pm. At 5:30 we will present the Volunteer of the Year award to Janet Conn, and then Carol Arthur from the Domestic Abuse Project will say a few words in honor of WATCH volunteers.

Please RSVP no later than October 11th to Mandy!

See the Inside of our County's Jail!
Join us on Tuesday, October 23rd for a tour of the Hennepin County Jail. Meet at the WATCH office at 11:00. The tour will start at 11:30 and be finished by 1:00. Don't miss this opportunity to see the inside of our county's jail, where in-custody defendants are held. RSVP to Mandy no later than October 18th.

WATCH's New Executive Director!

Please join us in welcoming Amy Arcand as the new Executive Director of WATCH. Amy will start November 1st and comes to WATCH with twelve years' experience as the executive director of neighborhood organizations on Minneapolis' north and south sides.

While in her current role as Executive Director of the Corcoran Neighborhood group, Amy co-created Women WISDOM, a neighborhood based outreach program to women used in prostitution, and Mujeres en Accion y Poder, a leadership program for Latinas. She recently began working with the Sexual Violence Center and their La Voz del Cambio prevention initiative. 
You will see Amy around the office, at court hearings, and at meetings beginning in a few weeks. We hope you'll make an effort to welcome her and share your knowledge and insights. We are very excited to welcome Amy to WATCH!
