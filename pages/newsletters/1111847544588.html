News, Reminders & Updates

WATCH's next volunteer training is scheduled for Saturday, February 2nd. Do you know someone who would make an excellent court monitor? Encourage them to contact Mandy and apply now!


If you sign up for a morning court shift, please remember to be at the office between 8:15 and 8:30 and return at the end of your shift no later than noon.
If you sign up for an afternoon court shift, please remember to be at the office between 12:45 and 1:00 and return at the end of your shift no later than 4:30.
Additionally, as WATCH is so fortunate to have an abundance of dedicated volunteers, please be sure that the shifts you sign up for will work with your schedule. If you're in doubt, wait to sign up so that you don't risk taking a shift from someone who is certain they are free!


Does WATCH have your correct contact information? Now that we're in the snowy season, we want to be certain we are able to contact you in the rare event that our office is closed. If your phone number or email have changed since you started as a volunteer, contact Mandy to update!
Happy Holidays from WATCH!

When it comes to our volunteers, WATCH could not be luckier. We appreciate you 365 days a year--we really do--but there's just something about the snowy roads (that you trek through to get here), and the start of a new year (which we hope you will spend with us), and the various forms of gift-giving (the greatest of which is the time you devote), that make this season especially appropriate to pause and say thank you for making WATCH a part of your lives.  

 

We send you wishes for safety, health, and smiles.  

 

And if you make it in to the office, we'll offer you chocolate!
